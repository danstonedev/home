// development & validation platform

simPAIN is built on PainMap.

PainMap is a measurement platform in development and validation for mapping pain in three dimensions — quality and spread, not just a 0–10 number4. It is available at pain3d.com for controlled pilot and research workflows; it is not a diagnostic device, and clinicians remain responsible for interpretation. simPAIN adapts the concept for the classroom.
