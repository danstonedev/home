Terms

The DevPT public website describes educational simulation tools for physical therapy education. The website is informational and may change as the project develops.

Educational use

DevPT is built for classroom simulation and training. It is not a substitute for clinical judgment, medical care, emergency evaluation, or supervision by qualified faculty or clinicians.

No real patient data

Do not enter protected health information or real patient data into public contact forms, email inquiries, demos, or simulation workflows unless a separate written agreement says otherwise.

Intellectual property

DevPT names, interface designs, screenshots, copy, and related materials are owned by their respective creators unless otherwise noted.
