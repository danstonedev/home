Privacy

DevPT's public website is designed to be informational. It does not ask visitors to create an account, upload patient information, or enter protected health information.

Information you choose to send

If you contact DevPT by email, the information in that email is used to respond to your inquiry and coordinate demos, collaboration, or research conversations.

Website data

The public homepage is a static site. Hosting providers may process basic technical logs such as IP address, browser, time of request, and requested URL for security, diagnostics, and reliability.

Student and patient data

Do not submit real patient data through the public website. DevPT classroom simulation is intended to use simulated cases and no real patient data.
